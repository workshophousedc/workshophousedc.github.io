Highlights from 2024

  • Singalongs: DC Singalong happens at Workshop every month or two—it’s a highly participatory event with lots of folk and pop songs and friendly faces, all welcome!
  • Parties: This year we hosted some very successful parties: Easter Puzzle Hunt, Casino Night, 4th of July & Halloween.
  • Fall Firepits: During October we lit our firepit every Sunday evening and had friends gather and vegan marshmallows to toast as the weather cooled down.
  • Vegan BBQ: We organized a pilgrimage to Terry’s Healthy Foods in Rockville, and then had a lovely vegan barbeque at the house.
  • Political Organizing: various political groups gathered here to plan, train their team, and make signs.
  • Launches: startup organizations InPress, Spiro, and Renaissance Philanthropy launched in the last year, all with connections to Workshop House!
  • Roof renovations: We redid our rooftop and HVAC systems at Workshop and are installing solar panels in the next few weeks. Special thanks to everyone who helped us clear the roof and carry stones!

Get Involved

We're community-builders at heart, and have two community initiatives where we are particularly looking for people excited to put in a bunch of work.

First is regular events. Workshop has a lot of one-off events, but we currently don't have anyone running recurring events, and we think repeated chill events have a ton of potential to nurture some neglected aspects of the community. So if you have ideas and/or energy to organize event series, please let Lincoln know.

Secondly, we're planning to co-organize a camp at Firefly in 2025, a mini Burning Man in the forest in Vermont. We need a few people who want to put some serious effort into planning a camp. You don't have to be an experienced Burner, but you do need to be willing to put a bunch of time in May and June. Let Lincoln know to what degree you would like to be involved.

Or, if you have other ideas, speak to Lincoln and we'll discuss!
